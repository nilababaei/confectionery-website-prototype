Our Cause

Our company helps in funding many charities and organizations nation-wide that aid in animal rescue, environmental conservation, and environmental sustainability. With every purchase our customers make, a dollar goes towards these amazing organizations, some including: The Animal Alliance of Canada, The Canadian Federation of Humane Societies, The Ottawa Humane Society, Canadian Parks and Wilderness Society, and many more.
